# Thermodynamics-B-Review-Handbook

This project is a LaTeX-based review handbook for **Thermodynamics B** at the **University of Science and Technology of China (USTC)**.

The handbook is organized around the 2026 Spring course materials and focuses on exam-oriented review: key concepts, formula frameworks, applicable conditions, problem-solving routes, and common pitfalls.

## Contents

- Chapter 1: Basic Concepts of Thermodynamics and Temperature
- Chapter 2: The First Law of Thermodynamics
- Chapter 3: The Second Law of Thermodynamics and Entropy
- Chapter 4: Maxwell–Boltzmann Distribution
- Chapter 5: Transport Processes in Gases
- Chapter 6: Properties of Solids and Liquids, and Phase Transitions
- Final Review Summary
